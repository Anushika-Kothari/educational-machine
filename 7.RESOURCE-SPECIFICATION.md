   * Radius(R) = 0.3 cm
   * Force(F) = m.g =  0.30 . 9.81 = 3 kg
   * Torque(T) = F.r = 3.0.3 = 0.9 kg/cm
   * Total Torque = T.f(Factor of safety) = 0.9 . 1.5 = 1.35 Nm
   * No-load Current = 800 mA .
